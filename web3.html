The unique colisium is at Rome in Italy

When you’re on a tour of Rome and set your eyes on the Colosseum for the first time, it’s only natural to ask yourself, “How old is the Roman Colosseum?” This marvel of ancient Roman engineering is over 1,900 years old. Construction began in 72 A.D. and finished in 80 A.D. That means it only took eight years to build. This Colosseum is inextricably tied to Rome’s cityscape, and traveler Simonette perfectly captured the feeling of experiencing all that history. “Seeing the city’s architecture, walls, arches, wells… and more that are thousands of years old? B.C. and A.D., it was awesome and surreal!! I still have chills just thinking about it.”
